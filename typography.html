Typefaces

Heading Typeface

  • Font Family: 'Josefin Sans'
    • Reason: Josefin Sans is chosen for headings due to its modern and stylish appearance, making it suitable for creating a contemporary and sleek design. Its geometric shapes and tall x-height lend a sense of sophistication, enhancing the visual impact of headings on the website.
  • Font Weight: 700 (Bold)
  • Font Size: 32px for desktop, 28px for mobile
  • Line Height: 1.2
  • Letter-spacing: 1px

Body Text Typeface

  • Font Family: Lato
    • Reason: Lato is highly readable in body texts, providing a comfortable reading experience for longer texts like articles or product descriptions. Its simplicity complements the elegance of the heading font.
  • Font Weight: 400 (Regular) for body, 700 (Bold) for important calls to action
  • Font Size: 16px for desktop, 14px for mobile
  • Line Height: 1.6
  • Letter-spacing: 0.5px
